Owen graduated with distinction from Temple University with majors in Political Science and German, but music has always remained his passion, and he continued to perform and study music alongside his undergraduate studies. Influenced by Philadelphia's long tradition as a center for jazz, he took up guitar and fretless bass, performing improvisation with notable local musicians at festivals and clubs. During a semester abroad in Leipzig, Germany, Owen was introduced to the hypnotizing sounds of techno and set out on a path to learn everything there is to know about electronic music production. He has a collection of vintage and contemporary synthesizers, drum machines and samplers on which he composes music using a variety of digital editing workstations.
